At the beginning of my first playtime at school, one of the more friendly white
girls led me into the playground, where all the others crowded around me. Two
boys started to back me
‘Blackie’
and
‘golliwog’.

This made me very upset. I remember taking my beret off my head and holding it
up to my face and saying,
‘Look, I’m not Black, this is!”

When I told the teacher she just said,
‘Take no notice.’

This sounded useless to me.
How come I not take any notice?
