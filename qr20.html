I remember being constantly asking by the teachers and the children where I came from,

what was it like,
did I live in a house or a tree,
did we wear clothes,
did we speak English?

You begin to feel so different, you feel uncomfortable, and because you are so young you don't know how to deal with it.

The way I dealt with it was I decided I wasn't going to fight it.
I gave in to whatever they said.
